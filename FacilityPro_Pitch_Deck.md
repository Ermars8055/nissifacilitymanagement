# FacilityPro: The Future of Spatial Facility Management

## 1. The Problem
Modern facilities are complex ecosystems. Yet, traditional facility management relies on disconnected spreadsheets, reactive maintenance logs, and 2D floor plans that fail to provide real-time situational awareness. 
- **Hidden Assets:** Technicians spend countless hours locating specific assets (HVACs, fire extinguishers, servers).
- **Reactive Firefighting:** Without intelligent scheduling, maintenance happens *after* critical equipment breaks down.
- **Data Silos:** Field workers and backend administrators operate on entirely different systems, leading to miscommunication and delayed complaint resolution.

## 2. Our Solution
**FacilityPro** is an end-to-end, cross-platform Facility Management System that bridges the gap between digital data and physical infrastructure. By combining an intuitive React-based Admin Dashboard with a powerful Flutter Mobile App, we put complete control in the hands of facility managers and ground technicians.

## 3. Key Differentiators & Core Features

### 🏢 Interactive 3D Spatial Mapping
We bring buildings to life. FacilityPro features a fully integrated **3D Engine** that allows managers to visualize buildings, isolate specific floors, and map real-world assets (like desks, fire alarms, and electrical panels) onto a 3D coordinate plane. 
- *Benefit:* Total spatial awareness. Know exactly where an asset is before dispatching a technician.

### 📱 Dynamic QR Code Integration
Every room and asset in the system generates a unique, scannable QR Code. 
- *Benefit:* A technician walks up to an AC unit, scans the physical QR code with our Mobile App, and instantly sees its maintenance history, upcoming PM schedules, and live status. 

### ⚙️ Preventive Maintenance (PM) & Ticketing
Shift from reactive to proactive. Our robust backend automatically schedules Preventive Maintenance (PM) routines with detailed checklists. 
- *Benefit:* Eliminate downtime. Ground workers receive mobile push notifications for due tasks, ensuring 100% compliance.

### 📊 Real-Time Admin Dashboard
A high-performance command center for executives and managers.
- *Benefit:* Track open complaints, monitor technician workloads, manage client portfolios (e.g., Apex Industries, Mercy Health), and oversee the entire hierarchy from a single pane of glass.

## 4. The Architecture (Built for Scale)
- **Mobile First:** Built with **Flutter**, ensuring a blazing-fast, native experience on both iOS and Android.
- **Enterprise Web:** An optimized **React Admin Panel** tailored for high-volume data management.
- **Robust Backend:** Powered by **ASP.NET Core (C#)** with Entity Framework, protected by Firebase JWT Authentication and Role-Based Access Control (RBAC).
- **OTA Ready:** Built-in Over-The-Air (OTA) update mechanics ensure that the workforce always has the latest tools without manual app store updates.

## 5. Target Market
- **Corporate Real Estate & Campuses:** Streamlining operations for multi-building complexes.
- **Healthcare Facilities:** Ensuring strict compliance and instant asset location (e.g., life-saving equipment).
- **Industrial & Manufacturing:** Managing heavy machinery maintenance schedules to prevent operational bottlenecks.
